=== ISLE OF DREAD — LORE DOCUMENT ===

The Isle of Dread sits in the far northeastern ocean, perpetually shrouded in fog.
Only seasoned adventurers dare approach its jagged shores.

History:
The Isle was once home to the Cult of the Void Wyrm, a sect of mages who
attempted to bind an ancient dragon named Vorynthas to their will.
The ritual failed. Vorynthas shattered the ritual circle, killing all seventeen
cultists. Their souls are said to still haunt the crumbling tower at the isle's
center, now known as the Tower of Regret.

Creatures:
- Void Wraiths (level 4-6, spectral, immune to poison)
- Corrupted Sea Drakes (level 5-7, fire breath, coastal zones)
- Vorynthas the Desolator (boss, level 12, fire + void breath, spawns weekly)

Treasure:
- Cultist Robe of the Void (artifact, +10 magery, 15% spell damage increase)
- Shard of the Shattered Circle (crafting reagent for high-level runic weapons)
- Vorynthas Scale (rare trophy, can be traded to Blacksmyth NPCs for unique recipes)

Access:
- Players must complete the "Echoes of the Void" quest chain first.
- The isle appears on the map only after speaking with the Cartographer NPC
  in Moonglow (located at 23N, 45W).
